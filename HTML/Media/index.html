What is it?

The FIFA World Cup is a international tournament between the best national soccer/football teams. The tournament is held every four years with 32 teams participationg. It was an idea proposed to create some sort of compeition between national teams.

Why does it matter

The FIFA World Cup has changed thousands of lives all aroundt the world. It creates a form of social conversation and bring people together. Not only that but it has drastically changed marketing and economic strategies between different companies as at least half the population watches the tournament. Lastly, FIFA has used it's big platform like the World Cup to spread different types of important messages and lifestyles.

Basic Rules

  1. Top 4 teams from all continents and other who qualify and win redemption games are brought to the tournament
  2. There are 3 group stage games, and 4 more knockout games, 7 games in total
  3. All games are 2 45 minute halfs, 90 minutes in total, in case of a tie there are 30 minutes of extra time, if game stays tied then it will be a penalty shootout (only in knockout stages)

2 Fun Facts

  1. The 1942 and 1956 World Cups did not happen due to WWII, it was brought back in 1950 after no one thought it would be able to make a comeback and reunited many people
  2. The World Cup was inspired by the Olympic Games, Jules Rimet thought there should be a international competition solely for soccer/football
